## Responsive Design

### Desktop (1920px+)

-   Stats cards: 4 kolom
-   Filter: 4 kolom
-   Tabel: Full width dengan semua kolom
-   Modal: Center, width 800px

### Tablet (768px - 1919px)

-   Stats cards: 2 kolom
-   Filter: 2 kolom
-   Tabel: Scrollable horizontal
-   Modal: Center, width 90%

### Mobile (< 768px)

-   Stats cards: 1 kolom (stacked)
-   Filter: 1 kolom (stacked)
-   Tabel: Scrollable horizontal
-   Modal: Full width, padding reduced
